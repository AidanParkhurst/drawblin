Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Drawblins collects and how it’s used.

1. Information we collect

  • Gameplay data: in-game actions and state required to run multiplayer sessions.
  • Optional account info: if you sign in, basic profile data from the provider (e.g., email, display name).
  • Purchase info: payment events are processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe). We receive confirmation of successful purchases, not your full card details.
  • Diagnostics: basic logs for stability and abuse prevention.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and improve gameplay and features.
  • To support purchases and entitlements.
  • To prevent abuse and maintain service integrity.
  • To communicate important changes or issues.

3. Sharing

We don’t sell your personal information. We share data only as needed to operate the service (e.g., with payment processors) or if required by law.

4. Data retention

We keep data only as long as necessary for the purposes described above.

5. Your choices

  • You can play as a guest without signing in.
  • You can request deletion or correction of your profile data by contacting us.

6. Children

Drawblins is not intended for children under 13. If you believe we’ve collected information from a child, contact us for removal.

7. Contact

Questions? Email support@drawbl.in.
